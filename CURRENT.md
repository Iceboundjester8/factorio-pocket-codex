## What this repo is
A structured export of your pocket Factorio journals, meant to be:
1) human-friendly on paper
2) file-friendly for git
3) later importable into an in-game manual system

## Operating rule
Every new note becomes **one page file** with a stable ID, then gets logged in:
- INDEX/MasterIndex.md (topic pointer)
- INDEX/IdRegistry.md (ID uniqueness)
- CHANGELOG.md (what changed)
